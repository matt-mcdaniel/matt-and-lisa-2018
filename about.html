About Old Arrowhead Ranch

Old Arrowhead Ranch (OAR) is located in Hunt, Texas.

Traveling to Kerrville, TX

Guests will be staying in Kerrville, TX, which is 15 miles away from the wedding venue in Hunt.

Kerrville is a 1 hour drive from the San Antonio International Airport. It's best to rent a car or carpool. Uber or Lyft may take you out there, but it's pretty far, so it's probably worth a courtesy call right after requesting.

Hotel Accommodations

Hotel arrangements should be made in Kerrville, Texas at the Holiday Inn Express, Best Western and Hampton Inn. Buses will provide transportation to and from the hotels in Kerrville and the venue in Hunt.
